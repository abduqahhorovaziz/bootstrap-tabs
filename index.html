Medical Examination Processing


Your visa application requires a medical exam. The Tashkent International Clinic will provide the results of your medical exam to the Embassy of the United States of America in Tashkent, Uzbekistan.


  • Step 1: Personal Information
  • Step 2: Payment
  • Step 3:Scheduling your appointment for a Medical Exam
  • Step 4:The day of your appointment

Step 1: Personal Information.

After reading through the steps and agreeing to the terms and conditions, continue to the next two screens to fill in all the needed personal information and upload your photo. Note: Before continuing onto the next screens. You will need the following:

  • Recent Electronic Photo 3 x 4 cm to upload (In color, white background, taken within the last six months)
  • Passport to complete personal information
  • E-mail address
  • Payment: MasterCard or Visa credit card

Step 2: Payment

After you have uploaded your photo and filled in all the necessary personal information you will be directed to the payment portal for IntMS (www.intms.org)  which automatically calculates the amount due for you individually or the total due for you and your family. Please Note:  TIC does not collect charges, the payment for medical exam directed to International Medical Services. The non-refundable cost of a medical exam and all required lab analysis is:
  • Applicants less than two years of age – $80.
  • Applicants 2 years but under 15 years of age – $90.
  • Applicants 15 years old between 18 years old – $165.
  • Applicants 18 years old between 24 years old – $205.
  • Applicants 24 years old between 44 years old – $180.
  • Applicants over 44 years old – $165.
You may pay the fee using a MasterCard or Visa
  • Make sure that the card holder’s name, card number and expiration date are correct.
  • Make sure there are enough funds on the payment card.
  • If your card is denied, check with the issuing bank of the card to find out why it is being denied
After making the payment a receipt and the information you have filled out will be sent to the e-mail address you specified in the form and to the Tashkent International Clinic.

Step 3: Scheduling your appointment for a Medical Exam

After you have received confirmation of payment, call the Tashkent International Clinic to schedule your appointment. Their phone number is: +998-71-291-0726 Calls are received between 8:30 am to 5:00 pm Monday through Friday.

Step 4: The day of your appointment

On the day of your appointment, arrive on time. Do not come early and do not be late. Bring the following documents with you:
  • Valid passport for each person in the family
  • 1 copy of the Passport(s) picture page
  • Recent Electronic Photo 3 x 4 cm (In color, white background, taken within the last six months)
  • Invitation letter for your interview at the US Embassy in Tashkent
  • Form DS-260 Immigrant Visa Electronic Application
  • All vaccination records; Form 063
  • Your address in the United States, your address in Uzbekistan, your e-mail address
